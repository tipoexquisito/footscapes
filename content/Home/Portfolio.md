## How I work

Four things travel with me from course to course, from Arouca to Arco Maior to Arrifana:

**Direção de cursos.** Coordinating pedagogical teams, Formação em Contexto de Trabalho (FCT) partnerships and Provas de Aptidão Profissional (PAP) is, at its core, an articulation job — making sure the pieces a course promises actually connect.

**FCT and PAP as real stakes, not paperwork.** A work placement or a final project only teaches something if it is treated as consequential. I visit, I question feasibility, I push back on plans that won't survive contact with a real workplace.

**Project-based learning with social-emotional learning built in, not bolted on.** At [[Arco Maior]], this is formalised through [[ROAD - Roteiro de Objetivos de Aprendizagem e Desenvolvimento|ROAD]] and the [[CASEL]] framework — competences assessed alongside disciplinary content, session by session, not as an afterthought.

**Differentiation as a default, not an accommodation.** Students at risk of dropout, a student on the autism spectrum, students newly arrived in Portugal — the starting move is the same: a safe space to experiment, at the student's own pace, that gradually widens what that student believes they can make and show.

## Selected projects

**[[Transforma-te Transforma|Transforma-te, Transforma]]** — Arco Maior, 2024/25. An 11-discipline project on bullying prevention, chosen by the students themselves. They built their own recording studio, designed the visual identity, and produced two podcast episodes for a real audience. Rated 5/5 on five of six [[21st Century Learning Design]] dimensions. The fullest account of how I work is here.

**Wiki de Cinematografia** — Agrupamento de Escolas de Arouca, 2022/23. A Notion-based repository built to teach audiovisual production, structured around pre-production, production, post-production and distribution, designed to adapt to different learning paces.

**Arouca Geofood** — Agrupamento de Escolas de Arouca, 2016/17. A finalist project in the Fundação Ilídio Pinho's Ciência na Escola award, connecting Multimedia, Cozinha/Pastelaria, Mesa/Bar and Turismo students around the culture and heritage of the Arouca Geopark.

**Laying European Table** — Agrupamento de Escolas de Arouca, Erasmus+, 2015–17. A two-year project linking schools in Italy, Latvia, Poland, Portugal and Turkey through European food culture, audiovisual production and entrepreneurship.
