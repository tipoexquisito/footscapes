I am a professional educator and trainer in the areas of design, communication and multimedia, with a background in communication design. My work focuses on creating learning experiences that are practical, participatory and grounded in real contexts.

Over the years, my practice has grown to include active methodologies, audiovisual production, digital literacy and project-based learning. I am interested in how these can work together to produce learning that connects people, disciplines and contexts. I am moving in the direction of Learning Experience Design: how, where, why and what it feels like to learn well.

Footscapes started as documentation of one week. An Erasmus+ KA1 mobility programme in Madrid and Toledo, focused on innovative and active methodologies for teaching and learning. It grew from there. This digital garden collects frameworks, reflections, artworks and field observations from that week and beyond. It is also a working portfolio of how I think and practise.

The underlying ideas are, more or less, simple: knowledge travels and history doesn't repeat itself, it rhymes.

— Filipe Gama
