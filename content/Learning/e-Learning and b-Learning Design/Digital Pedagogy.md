# e-/b-Learning Design for Basic and Secondary Education
_A section in development._

This section grows from a different kind of mobility. Not geographical, but methodological.

The Portuguese teacher training programme "Conceção de Atividades em e-/b-Learning para os Ensinos Básico e Secundário" asks a different set of questions. Not where does learning happen, but how do we design it when the walls disappear. When the classroom becomes asynchronous. When the educator becomes an architect of digital experience.

Some frameworks travel from [[Learning/Active Methodologies - Teaching and Learning/index|Active Methodologies for Teaching and Learning]]: [[Universal Design for Learning|UDL]],[[Scaffolding]], [[AI Literacy]]. Others are specific to this territory: instructional design models, blended learning structures and the particular challenges of online pedagogy.

This section is still finding its shape. Some notes already exist. Others are seeds.
