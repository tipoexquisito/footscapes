# Reading List

The bibliography below grounds the theoretical framework of [[e-b-Learning Design for Basic and Secondary Education]]. It spans distance education theory, learning management systems, personal learning environments, communities of practice, and online course design.

Key anchors: Siemens on connectivism and networked learning ecology; Peres and Pimenta on b-learning theory and practice in Portuguese educational contexts; Stavredes on effective online teaching; Harasim on learning networks. Alongside these, a strand of reflection on the Personal Learning Environment (Attwell, Gibb, Farmer, Milligan) runs through the bibliography as a recurring question about what learners carry beyond any particular platform.

Siemens, G. (2007). PLEs — I Acronym, Therefore I exist. *elearnspace*.

Stavredes, T. (2011). *Effective online teaching: foundations and strategies for student success*. San Francisco: Jossey-Bass.
