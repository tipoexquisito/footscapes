# Session 9 — Practical Cases

**Date:** 21 May 2026 · **Format:** Online (asynchronous), 18:00–20:00 · **Facilitator:** Daniela Mascarenhas

The closing content session moved from frameworks to institutional practice, through two case studies.

The first examined a teacher-training project developed in São Tomé and Príncipe, with World Bank support, structured as a sequence of modules (covering areas such as ICT, Mathematics, Portuguese and pedagogical practice) distributed across an academic year and alternating between face-to-face and online weeks.

The second walked through "Paula Frassinetti / CEaD," a Moodle-based LMS used in Portuguese teacher education, looking at how it organises lesson plans, guides, weekly structures, scheduled (and at times intentionally hidden) assessments, and video resources. The session used both cases to anchor the frameworks of earlier sessions in the operational reality of running an e-/b-learning programme.
