# Session 3 — Learning Management Systems: From Environments to Models

**Date:** 19 March 2026 · **Format:** Online (asynchronous), 18:00–21:00 · **Facilitator:** Mário Cruz

The session compared virtual learning environments, content management systems and learning management systems, and introduced Stephen Downes's 2005 critique that conventional LMS architectures may no longer match how people actually learn.

Two platforms anchored the discussion. Moodle, an open-source LMS, was presented through its forums, quizzes, multimedia support and active user community. TalentLMS, a cloud-based alternative, was discussed for its gamification features, integrated video-conferencing, and scalability (unlimited users and storage). The session connected platform choice to collaborative learning design and to how assessment is embedded within an LMS.

> [!note] Task 3 — Review quiz on Wayground
> A gamified quiz on [Wayground](https://wayground.com), covering the combined content of Sessions 1 to 3. Participants completed the quiz from the learner's perspective; a later task would return to Wayground from the teacher's side, building content rather than answering it.
