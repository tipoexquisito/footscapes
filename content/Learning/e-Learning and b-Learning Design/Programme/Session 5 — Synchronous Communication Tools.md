# Session 5 — e/b-learning Kit I: Synchronous Communication Tools

**Date:** 23 April 2026 · **Format:** Online (synchronous), 18:00–20:30 · **Facilitator:** Mário Cruz

The fifth session surveyed the main synchronous communication platforms, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Skype, and Internet Relay Chat, and the features that distinguish them: text and video chat, breakout rooms, screen sharing, recorded audio and video messages.

Particular attention went to live-polling tools such as Poll Everywhere and Anywhere Polls, framed as ways of converting a one-directional presentation into a real conversation. The session also examined chat as a "hybrid genre" that follows neither the conventions of speech nor those of writing, and discussed how it enables dialogic negotiation in real time.

> [!note] Task 5 — Timed quiz
> A 30-minute timed online quiz, opened once participants began it, checking retention of the session's content on synchronous communication tools.
