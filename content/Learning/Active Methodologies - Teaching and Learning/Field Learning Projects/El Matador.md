# Context4Content, Lowercase

The evening was cañas and tapas at El Matador, near Sol.

The coordinator had confirmed in the morning what the week had been built on: culture at two registers. The Prado and the plaza. The paintings and the semáforos. The guided visits and the unguided evenings. Both intentional. Both part of the [[Context4Content]] design.

El Matador near Sol was the lowercase end of the same sentence that had started at the Prado.
