Dominican friar and painter, c. 1395–1455, Florence. Fra Angelico worked within a devotional tradition but brought to it a quality of spiritual clarity and compositional intelligence that has influenced painters across five centuries.

[[La Anunciación]] in the Prado is one of his most complete works and a key example of early Renaissance spatial organisation and theological programme. The garden of Eden appears in the left panel — the Fall is depicted alongside the Annunciation, the beginning of salvation.
