Flemish painter, c. 1400–1464, and one of the most influential artists of the Northern Renaissance. His work is characterised by emotional intensity, detailed drapery, and a precise attention to grief.

[[El Descendimiento]] (The Descent from the Cross), c. 1435, is the masterpiece of the Prado's Flemish collection. The painting depicts the removal of Christ's body from the cross with extraordinary psychological precision: each figure's grief is individualised and yet part of a collective choreography of mourning.
