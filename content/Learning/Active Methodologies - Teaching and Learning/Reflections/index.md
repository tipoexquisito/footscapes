# Reflections

The sessions ended. The thinking continued.

These notes are not summaries of what was learned. They are the places where the week kept working — in corridors and parks and paintings, in questions that arrived after the fact, in the gap between what was said and what it meant. Some are short. Some are essays. [[Fiat Humanitas]] is the longest, written after the Reina Sofía, after everything.

The register here is different from the programme notes or the framework references. Less explanatory. More willing to stay with difficulty.
