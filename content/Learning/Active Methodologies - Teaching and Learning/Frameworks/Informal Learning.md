# Informal Learning

Informal learning occurs outside formal educational structures — without prescribed learning objectives, structured curricula or formal assessment. It happens in markets, bars, parks, conversations, accidental encounters with objects and places.

It is not unintentional learning. It is learning that does not require a timetable to begin.

This course was designed to take informal learning seriously as a pedagogical category. The walk through El Rastro before the course began. The Metropolis projection at Sala Equis. The walk through Casa de Campo with no task card. The sticker culture observed on Madrid street signs. None of these were in the official programme. All of them were part of the learning.

> [!connection]
> Informal learning is the lowercase register of [[Context4Content]]. The canónico is the Prado. The informal is the bocadillo de calamares. Both are intentional. Both teach.
