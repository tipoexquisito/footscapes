- Now find a date for every hour on the clock.
- Try not to repeat partners (but if necessary you can)
- Now sit down and wait for instruction.

---

- Are you free at 1 o’clock?
- Can we meet at 1 o’ clock?
- When are you free?
- Do you have any hour free?
- **Let’s have a date!**

---

### It’s 1 o´clock.
How many years have you been teaching?
### It’s 2 o´clock.
What made you participate in an Erasmus+ Project?
### It’s 3 o´clock.
What subject do you teach and what level?
### It’s 4 o´clock.
How would you describe your teaching method?
### It’s 5 o´clock.
Do your students use digital devices in the classroom?
### It’s 6 o´clock.
Does your school encourage you to use any particular methodology?
### It’s 7 o´clock.
How do you feel about exams?
### It’s 8 o´clock.
What kind of learner are you / were you when you were a student?
### It’s 9 o´clock.
What is the one big change you would like to make in your teaching?
### It’s 10 o´clock.
Name some methodologies or approaches to teaching.
### It’s 11 o´clock.
What are your students’ biggest challenges?
### It’s 12 o´clock.
What are your biggest challenges as a teacher?
