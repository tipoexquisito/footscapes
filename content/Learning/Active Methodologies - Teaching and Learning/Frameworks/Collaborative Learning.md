# Collaborative Learning

Collaborative learning describes learning that emerges from joint intellectual effort — shared inquiry, co-construction of meaning, mutual accountability for outcomes.

It overlaps with [[Cooperative Learning]] but the emphasis differs. Cooperative learning foregrounds structure: assigned roles, task interdependence, individual accountability within the group. Collaborative learning foregrounds emergence: what becomes possible when minds work together that would not be possible alone.

In practice, the two are complementary. The structures of cooperative learning create the conditions for genuine collaboration to occur.
