This exists when students perceive they are linked with each other in a way that one cannot succeed unless **everyone** succeeds. If one fails, they all fail. It creates commitment and is at the heart of Cooperative Learning.

> “We need each other to succeed.”

---

- ninguém tem sucesso sozinho;
- se um falha, todos falham;
- compromisso coletivo.
