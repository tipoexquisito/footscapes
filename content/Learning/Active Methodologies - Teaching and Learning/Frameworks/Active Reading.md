Rather than passive reading of a text, students can be asked to perform specific cognitive operations:

- Summarize
- Understand
- Translate
- Quote
- Paraphrase
- Make connections
- Make associations
- Explain
- Exemplify
- Hypothesize

Each is a different cognitive operation and a different language task. Choosing which one to require is itself a pedagogical decision about what kind of thinking the lesson is developing. A CLIL teacher does not assign reading. They assign an operation to perform on the text.
