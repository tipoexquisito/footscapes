1. [[Multiple Means of Engagement]]
   Stimulate motivation and sustained enthusiasm for learning by promoting various ways of engaging with material.

2. [[Multiple Means of Representation]]
   Present information and content in a variety of ways to support understanding by students with different learning styles/abilities.

3. [[Multiple Means of Action or Expression]]
   Offer options for students to demonstrate their learning in various ways (e.g. allow choice of assessment type).
