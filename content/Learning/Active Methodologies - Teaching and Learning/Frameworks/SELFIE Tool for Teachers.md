SELFIE (Self-reflection on Effective Learning by Fostering the use of Innovative Educational technologies) is a free, customisable self-reflection tool developed by the European Commission to help educators assess their digital competence.

SELFIE for Teachers is distinct from the school-level SELFIE tool. It focuses on the individual educator's digital practices across teaching, learning and professional development.

The tool is aligned with [[DigCompEdu]] and covers areas including:
- use of digital tools in classroom practice
- digital assessment and feedback
- creating and adapting digital resources
- professional learning and collaboration
- supporting learners' digital competence

SELFIE for Teachers is not a test. It produces a personal profile — a starting point for reflection, not a score to be optimised.
