A brief sensory presence exercise. Entry point for grounding practice at the start of a session or as a transition between activities.

---

## The Invitation

Before we begin, take a moment to arrive.

Notice where you are: the temperature of the room, the weight of your body in the chair, the light coming through the window.

Notice what you can hear: near sounds first, then further away.

Notice what you can see: without moving your head, expand your peripheral vision.

You are here. This is where learning begins.

## Toledo as Invitation

The same invitation applies to a city. Toledo — walked slowly, with attention — is a mindfulness exercise at architectural scale. The walls, the light, the layers of stone, the sound of the *casco histórico* — all of it available to anyone who slows down enough to actually look.

See [[Toledo — The City of Three Cultures]] and [[Place-based Learning]].
