This activity is effective for reading long and challenging texts. 
The students take turns to have one of three roles: reader, summariser and corrector. 
The roles are rotated as they continue through the text, sentence by sentence or paragraph by paragraph depending on the level of the students.

|                        | Student 1                          | Student 2                          | Student 3                          | Student 4                          |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **1st sentence/chunk** | READ                               | SUMMARIRE<br>REPHRASE<br>TRANSLATE | CORRECT                            | CORRECT                            |
| **2nd sentence/chunk** | CORRECT                            | READ                               | SUMMARIRE<br>REPHRASE<br>TRANSLATE | CORRECT                            |
| **3rd sentence/chunk** | CORRECT                            | CORRECT                            | READ                               | SUMMARIRE<br>REPHRASE<br>TRANSLATE |
| **4th sentence/chunk** | SUMMARIRE<br>REPHRASE<br>TRANSLATE | CORRECT                            | CORRECT                            | READ                               |

---

### Funções:

- reader
- summariser
- corrector

Rotação de papéis.

Usado para textos longos/difíceis.
