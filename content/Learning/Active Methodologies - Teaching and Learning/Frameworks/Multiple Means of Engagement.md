Stimulating students' interests and motivation for learning in a variety of ways:

- Share learning goals
- Create and follow classroom routines
- Know their strengths and weaknesses
	- Give choice
	- Expect change
	- Never **assume**
- Silent and mindful moments
- Flexible classrooms (comfort, atmosphere)
- Reflection and feedback tools (Exit tickets)
