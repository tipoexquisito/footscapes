[[Learning/Active Methodologies - Teaching and Learning/index|Active Methodologies for Teaching and Learning]] is an Erasmus+ KA1 structured course organised by [[English Matters]], held across Madrid and Toledo, 3 to 9 May 2026. It sits within a broader European commitment to professional development for educators, framed by the [[European Dimension in Education|Notes on European Dimension]] and certified under Erasmus+ quality standards. Forty hours across seven days. Participants were educators. For that week, they were also learners. The distinction mattered.
