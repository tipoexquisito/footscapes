# Patrícia Portela

Portuguese writer and dramaturgist working across the borders of literature, theatre and performance. Her prose resists linear reading; her books are built to be entered from more than one point, closer to a score than to a plot.

[[Hoje, 3 de Maio]] (2024) takes [[Goya]]'s painting of the Madrid executions and turns it into exactly that kind of object: a novel with no single order of reading.
