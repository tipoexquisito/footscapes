**Fiat Humanitas** traces a single lineage of witness across two centuries: Goya's *2 de mayo* and *3 de mayo*, Picasso's *Guernica*, Dumile Feni's *African Guernica*, Mohammed Al-Hawajri's *Guernica–Gaza*. History doesn't repeat itself. It rhymes, and each artist who picks up the rhyme is answering the same question this garden keeps asking: what does it mean to be an educator today, and what will we need now and in the future?

The essay is not a page of this garden. It is a separate, standalone piece.
