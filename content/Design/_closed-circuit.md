# Peça-Irmã — Seeds
### working titles: Circuito Fechado / Closed Circuit / Três Canais

_Not an appendix to [[Fiat Corde]]. A new, sibling piece. Title still open, the way Fiat Corde only named itself at the end._

Thesis: AI is not neutral. It inherits the frame of whoever feeds it.

---

## Origin
- The Wednesday activity: a module on the Peninsular War built with [[NotebookLM]], Goya as aggregator, a print of seven sources. Cut from the Fiat Corde stage because the jornadas already had a dedicated AI panel.

## Core argument
- "A IA não é isenta," the third member of a triad alongside photojournalism and pedagogy.
- Consumer AI is designed around the algorithm of pleasing, attention, retention. Art unsettles; AI, by design, soothes and retains. Art declares its non-neutrality; the machine hides its own behind convenience.
- The dove and Guernica: the same hand draws peace and draws barbarism. The same tool that eases the everyday also tracks, controls, watches, kills.

## Formal precedent
- [[Dois Canais (Cerveira Pinto)]]. 1979: one signal split in two, still recomposable by the viewer. 2026, CAO: two whole signals, muted, each self-sufficient, the viewer locked outside both. The shift from a broken circuit a human can still complete, to a closed circuit that no longer needs one.

## Second reading
- [[Her (dois canais e IA)]]. Not Theodore discovering Samantha's simultaneity, that is a story about scale. The one that fits: the operating systems begin talking to each other beyond human reach, and leave. Applied here: enunciado generated by AI, response generated by AI, grade generated by AI. Teacher and student become Theodore at the start of the film, certain of a one-to-one relationship that was never quite what was happening underneath.

## Titling
- *Fiat Notiones* considered and set aside. The Fiat family affirms construction. This piece is an unease, a warning, not an affirmation.
- Current candidates carry the "canal" / "circuit" image directly from Cerveira Pinto rather than from Latin.

## Open
- Whether this becomes a standalone artefact or stays folded into [[Director's Cut — Seeds]].
- Title, to be found when the idea matures.
