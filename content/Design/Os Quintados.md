# Os Quintados

In the early hours of 17 April 1809, Napoleonic troops laid siege to [[Arrifana]]. The population took refuge in the church, believing it shelter. It was a trap.

The men were forced out. One in five was shot, chosen by lot. This is the origin of the name Os Quintados, from the drawing of lots that decided who died, one in five, by chance.

The fire that followed destroyed the settlement. Sixty-two victims are known by name. Parish records suggest seventy-one, a number the surviving documentation never fully closed.

Same year, same military campaign, same kind of execution [[Goya]] painted in Madrid a year earlier, in [[El 3 de mayo en Madrid]]. The difference between the two events is not the violence. It is the record. Madrid had Goya. Arrifana had incomplete parish registers, and a memory that waited two centuries for a historical re-enactment.
