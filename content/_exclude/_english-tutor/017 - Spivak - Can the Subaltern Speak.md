## The story behind it

Spivak wrote this as a dense, difficult intervention into postcolonial theory. It asks a simple question with a devastating answer: can those outside the dominant system ever truly speak within it?

Full text: [[Spivak - Can the Subaltern Speak - Text]]

## Why this text, why now

You are designing learning experiences for students from contexts very different from the ones that produced most instructional design theory. This text will not let you forget that.
