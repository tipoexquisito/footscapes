## The story behind it

Benjamin wrote this as fascism was rising across Europe and images were becoming weapons of mass political persuasion. He wanted to understand what happens to meaning when anything can be copied.

Full text: [[Benjamin - The Work of Art in the Age of Mechanical Reproduction - Text]]

## Why this text, why now

Every asset you place inside a digital learning experience is a reproduction. This text asks what that costs.
