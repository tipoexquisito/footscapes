## The story behind it

Woolf delivered this as two lectures at Cambridge in 1928, at colleges that had only recently admitted women. She began by talking about lunch and ended by talking about freedom.

Full text: [[Woolf - A Room of One's Own - Text]]

## Why this text, why now

The conditions for learning are not neutral. This text will make you audit every learning environment you design for who it was built for.
