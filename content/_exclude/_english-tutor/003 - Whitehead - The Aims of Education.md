## The story behind it
Whitehead delivered this as a lecture in 1916, in the middle of a war that was killing the generation he was trying to educate. He argued that dead knowledge was more dangerous than ignorance.

Full text: [[Whitehead - The Aims of Education - Text]]

## Why this text, why now
Every microlearning module you will ever design lives or dies on Whitehead's three rhythms: romance, precision, generalisation.
