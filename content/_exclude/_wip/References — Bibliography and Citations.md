APA 7th edition references for frameworks, books, articles, and quotations cited across Footscapes. Organised by section.

---

## How to cite in notes

For inline citations within a note, use the author-year format in parentheses: (Vygotsky, 1978) or (Johnson & Johnson, 1994). For direct quotations, add the page number where known: (Schön, 1983, p. 54).

For longer quotations, use an Obsidian blockquote:

```
> "The quill is the tongue of the soul."
> — Cervantes, *Don Quixote* (1605)
```

## APA 7th edition quick reference

**Book:**
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title in italics*. Publisher.

**Chapter in edited book:**
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In E. Editor (Ed.), *Title of book* (pp. xx–xx). Publisher.

**Journal article:**
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Name, volume*(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xxxxx

**Website or online document:**
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). *Title of page*. Site Name. URL

**Image or artwork (museum):**
Artist Last Name, First Initial. (Year). *Title of work* [Medium]. Museum Name, City. URL

**Photograph (personal):**
Photographer Last Name, First Initial. (Year). *Description* [Photograph]. Context or publication.
